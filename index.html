proofie.

UX writing audit, right inside Figma.

proofie reviews the copy in your Figma designs for tone, accessibility, consistency, and localization — then suggests rewrites you can apply in place. Every suggestion is grounded against your own style guide and cites its source, so you can trust what it flags.

  • Four audits in one pass: tone · accessibility · consistency · localization
  • Retrieval-augmented against the style guide you upload — not generic advice
  • Every finding cites the rule it came from
  • Apply fixes in place, with one-click undo
  • English & Brazilian Portuguese interface
